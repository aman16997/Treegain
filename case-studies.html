Hello! I am Clover

Clover is a credit card company founded by people who were tired of bureaucracy in the fintech. In short, it's a company aiming to help people succeed financially while building their credit history. ‍

Our experience was brought from the world biggest financial institutions and the most innovative startups to create a product that we’d like to use ourselves.
