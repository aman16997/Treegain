Our route

Outcrowd has conducted the following steps in order to achieve the goal:
Conducted polls among the people who use online banking in their daily life to extract the issues of the existing systems.

Made several user testings of the beta version during the project creation (on the group of people who work in the non-financial spheres) to make sure the experience we’ve created is straightforward and easy to understand for average users. Analyzed the UX of banking processes and brainstormed ideas that can enhance the current flows in existing services.

Made the cards comparison with other banks. Developed unique branding that reflects the company’s main goals, strategy, and covers audience’s needs.
